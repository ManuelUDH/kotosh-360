National Geographic-style composition revealing the complete temple architecture in a single frame.

DETAILED ARCHITECTURE:
The sacred chamber has a nearly square rectangular floor plan, surrounded by massive mud and adobe walls approximately 1 meter thick, with exterior and interior surfaces cracked in irregular patterns from decades of drying. Along the interior walls, multiple trapezoidal and rectangular openings of different sizes are distributed — from narrow access doors to small offering niches — cut into the thick walls like windows carved in earth. National Geographic archaeological documentary photography style, hyper-detailed, tack-sharp focus throughout the scene, 8K resolution, Unreal Engine 5 render quality with Lumen global illumination and Nanite micro-geometry. Absolute historical and archaeological accuracy. 16:9 cinematic aspect ratio, wide-angle lens capturing the full breadth of the sacred enclosure. --ar 16:9
